How It Works

Institutes Raise Requirements

Orphanages, elderly homes, and other charitable institutions submit their needs for groceries and essential items.

Donors Choose to Help

Donors select specific items or amounts to contribute, ensuring their support goes directly to needed items.

Local Suppliers Fulfill Orders

Nearby shops receive orders, with payments directly transferred to them for transparency.

Items Delivered to Institutes

Suppliers pack and deliver items directly to the institutes, completing the transparent cycle.

Our Commitment to Transparency

No Direct Money Transfer

Funds are sent directly to suppliers, ensuring donations are used for intended purposes.

Anti-Cartelization Measures

Large donations are distributed among different shops, and suppliers are rotated to prevent monopolization.

Consumption Monitoring

System tracks consumption patterns and flags anomalies to prevent misuse of resources.

Quality Feedback

Institutes provide feedback on received items to maintain quality standards.

About Handout to Needy

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind our initiative

Our Mission

At Handout to Needy, we're committed to building a transparent ecosystem that connects charitable institutions, compassionate donors, and trustworthy suppliers to ensure resources reach those who need them most efficiently.

We believe that transparency is the foundation of trust, which is why we've built a platform that allows for complete visibility into the donation process - from the initial request to the final delivery.

Our Story

2022

The Beginning

Our founder witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by orphanages in receiving consistent support. Despite many willing donors, there was no transparent system to connect needs with resources.

2023

Platform Development

We assembled a team of developers and social workers to create a platform that would bridge this gap, focusing on transparency and accountability.

2024

Initial Launch

Handout to Needy was launched in 5 cities across India, connecting 20 institutions with local donors and suppliers.

2025

Nationwide Expansion

Today, we operate across India, helping hundreds of institutions receive the support they need through our transparent ecosystem.

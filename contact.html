Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a donor?

Becoming a donor is simple! Just click on "Become a Donor" or "Donor Registration" and fill out the registration form. Once registered, you can browse institutes' needs and contribute.

How do I know my donation reaches those in need?

Our platform ensures transparency by sending your donation directly to verified suppliers, who then deliver goods to institutes. You'll receive updates and can track the entire process.

What types of institutes can register on your platform?

We welcome orphanages, elderly homes, shelters, and other charitable institutions that serve people in need. All institutes undergo a verification process before being approved.

How do you verify suppliers?

Suppliers must provide business documentation, undergo a verification process, and maintain quality standards. We regularly collect feedback from institutes about the goods received.
